The man who
started it all

In 1980 our founder, Fred B. Fiske, began a small “pipe covering” shop, specializing in insulation of piping, duct, tanks, and boilers. Over many years and thousands of jobs, M/0 has become one of the largest insulation contractors in the world. M/0 operates from branches in RI, MA and CT. Power plants, schools, freezer facilities, office buildings, hospitals, paper mills, fishing boats, pharmaceutical plants, shipyards, builders, architects, homeowners and more rely on M/0 as a valuable resource for the best insulation and refractory work. We work directly for many commercial and industrial facility owners and managers. Maintenance chiefs, plant managers, and facility engineers have become loyal repeat customers because they know we provide quality workmanship, done safely, on time and within budget. Secondly, we are frequently subcontractors for “commercial work”, hired by HVAC, plumbing and mechanical contractors to insulate their projects. The same attributes of quality, reliability, safety and fair prices have earned Anchor a long list of references in the commercial work as well.

M/0’s diversification into building and home insulation services brought similar success in a new trade: building insulation. Here we serve two customer bases as well: Homeowners and builders/developers. Again, commitment to quality work, done safely, on schedule and at fair prices resulted in numerous homeowners and builders that appreciate M/0. In residential and building insulation, M/0’s team installs fiberglass and cellulose insulation in homes and buildings of all sizes. Additionally, as licensed Icynene spray foam dealers, we can insulate and air seal your home or building to dramatically reduce heating and cooling costs. With a long list of very happy customers, M/0 has become one of the nation’s leading spray foam contractors.
